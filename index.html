Why Privacy Matters

Your Wardrobe Data
Never Leaves Your Device

Most fashion apps upload your photos to the cloud, build profiles, and share data with advertisers. FitWardrobe is different — our AI runs entirely on your phone. Your clothing photos, body measurements, and style preferences are processed locally and never transmitted anywhere.

We built FitWardrobe this way because we believe personal data should stay personal. No servers to hack, no databases to breach, no profiles to sell. Just intelligent fashion advice that respects your privacy.
