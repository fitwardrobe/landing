AI Integration: Use FitWardrobe inside ChatGPT

Transform your styling conversations with the upcoming FitWardrobe + ChatGPT integration. Access your private digital wardrobe directly from the world's most powerful AI.

At FitWardrobe, we aren't just building an app; we are building an **ecosystem for private fashion intelligence**. Our goal is to make your wardrobe accessible wherever you need styling advice—starting with a deep integration into ChatGPT and other Large Language Models (LLMs).

The Future: A Custom FitWardrobe GPT

In the coming months, we will launch the **Official FitWardrobe Custom GPT**. This integration will allow you to ask ChatGPT questions like:

"Hey ChatGPT, I'm going to a wedding in Goa tomorrow. Look through my FitWardrobe and suggest 3 outfits that would be appropriate."

By connecting your local wardrobe data to the Custom GPT, you get high-level reasoning from OpenAI combined with the specific, private facts of your own closet.

Our "Open API" Concept

We believe that "Data Sovereignty" and "Interoperability" are not mutually exclusive. We are designing an **Open API for Personal Fashion** that allows you to authorize specific AI tools to access a temporary, sanitized snapshot of your wardrobe data.

  • Granular Control: You choose exactly which categories (e.g., only "Shoes" or only "Formal Wear") an AI can see.
  • One-Time Access: Grants can be set to expire immediately after a styling session.
  • Standardized Schema: Our JSON-LD based wardrobe format ensures that any future AI—whether it's Claude, Gemini, or a specialized styling bot—can understand your collection perfectly.

Privacy-Safe AI Routing

The technical challenge of AI integration is maintaining the privacy that FitWardrobe is known for. Our solution is **Privacy-Safe Routing**:

  1. Local Pre-Processing: Your phone's local AI handles the images. The external LLM (like ChatGPT) never sees your raw photos.
  2. Metadata Anonymization: Our secure proxy strips your name, location, and identifiable background details before the LLM receives your wardrobe list.
  3. Secure Tunnels: Communications between the FitWardrobe ecosystem and external AI providers are encrypted end-to-end and authenticated via your Supabase account.

Security Note

We will never implement an integration that requires the permanent storage of your wardrobe photos on third-party servers. Your privacy remains our #1 technical requirement.

Why This Matters

Integrating with LLMs turns your digital wardrobe from a simple list into a **proactive styling assistant**. You'll be able to plan your month's outfits, generate packing lists for complex trips, and get instant "cost-per-wear" justifications for new purchases—all through a natural chat interface.

Stay Updated

Development of the FitWardrobe GPT and Open API is currently in private alpha. We are working closely with early testers to ensure the routing is performance-optimized and 100% secure.
