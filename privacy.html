Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

FitWardrobe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Data Collection and Usage

FitWardrobe operates on a "Local First" principle. This means:

  • Photos: Your wardrobe photos are stored locally on your device. We do not upload them to cloud servers for processing.
  • Wardrobe Data: Your categorization and item data reside in your device's local storage.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use anonymous analytics to improve the app stability, but no personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at fitwardrobee@gmail.com.
