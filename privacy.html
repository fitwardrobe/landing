Introduction

FitWardrobe ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and web service (collectively, the "Service").

Our Core Principle: Your wardrobe data stays on your device. We believe your personal fashion choices are yours alone.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Email address (for authentication only)
  • Account creation date
  • Login method (email/magic link or Google OAuth)

That's it. We do NOT collect:

  • Your name, photo, or location
  • Your payment information (app is free)
  • Your wardrobe data, clothing photos, or outfit history
  • Any personal fashion information

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

Usage Data (Anonymous) via Plausible & Sentry:

  • Device type, App version, OS version
  • Crash reports (anonymized)
  • General usage statistics (privacy-focused)

We do NOT collect: IP addresses (anonymized), precise location, or device identifiers.

1.3 Data Stored Locally (On Your Device)

The following data is stored ONLY on your device (IndexedDB/filesystem):

  • Wardrobe items and photos
  • Outfit history and style preferences

This data NEVER leaves your device unless you explicitly export it or use AI features (where only the temporary photo is analyzed).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication (Supabase): Email used solely for secure login.
  • AI Features (Google Gemini): Photos sent temporarily for analysis. Google does NOT store your images.
  • Crash Reports (Sentry): Anonymous error data to fix bugs.
  • Analytics (Plausible): Anonymous usage stats to improve the app. No cookies or tracking.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information.

Limited data is shared with infrastructure providers (Supabase, Google, Sentry, Plausible, Vercel) solely for app functionality, subject to their strict privacy policies.

4. Data Storage & Security

On Your Device: Wardrobe data, photos, settings.

On Our Servers: Email address only (encrypted).

We use HTTPS encryption, secure Supabase authentication (passwordless/OAuth), and protected API keys.

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access/Export: Download all your data as JSON via Settings.
  • Delete: Delete your account anytime. Email and ID are removed; local data remains until you clear it.
  • Opt-out: Plausible is privacy-friendly, but you can use ad-blockers.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at: fitwardrobee@gmail.com
