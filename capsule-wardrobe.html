The Concept

What Is a Capsule Wardrobe?

A capsule wardrobe is a small, curated collection of essential clothing items that work together in many combinations. Coined by Susie Faux in the 1970s, the idea is simple: own fewer, better items and always have something to wear.

The ideal capsule wardrobe typically contains 30–40 versatile pieces — basics in neutral colours that mix and match effortlessly, with a few statement pieces for character. When done right, you can create hundreds of distinct outfits from a handful of items.

Benefits

Why Build a Capsule Wardrobe?

  • Save time — stop spending 20 minutes deciding what to wear every morning
  • Save money — buy less, but better quality items that last longer
  • Reduce decision fatigue — fewer choices means more mental energy for things that matter
  • Travel lighter — a capsule wardrobe fits in a carry-on and works for any destination
  • More sustainable — buying fewer, longer-lasting clothes reduces your fashion footprint
  • Always feel put-together — every item in your capsule works with everything else
