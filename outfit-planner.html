The Problem

Why You Feel Like You Have Nothing to Wear

Research shows that most people wear only 20% of their wardrobe 80% of the time. The other 80% of clothes sit untouched — not because they are bad, but because you cannot see how they fit together. FitWardrobe solves this with AI.

How It Works

Plan Your Week in Three Steps

1

Photograph Your Clothes

Take photos of every item in your wardrobe. FitWardrobe's AI identifies the type, colour, and style — no manual tagging required. Your photos stay on your device.

2

Tell It Where You're Going

Select your occasion — work, casual, dinner, gym, travel — and FitWardrobe generates complete outfit combinations from your own clothes that match the vibe.

3

Plan Your Whole Week

Lay out Monday to Friday outfits in one go. Spend less time deciding what to wear — your week is planned, your clothes are ready, your mornings are more intentional.

Features

AI Outfit Planning Features

Weekly Outfit Planning

Plan all 7 days at once. Swipe through AI-generated outfit options and lock in your look for each day.

Occasion-Based Suggestions

Work meeting, first date, gym session, beach holiday — tell FitWardrobe where you're going and it picks the right outfit.

Style Learning

The more you use FitWardrobe, the better it understands your preferences. Rate outfits and it refines suggestions to better match your style.

Privacy

Your Outfits Stay Private

Most outfit planner apps require you to upload your photos to their servers. FitWardrobe does not. Everything is processed on your device. Your wardrobe data, your outfit history, your style preferences — none of it ever leaves your phone.
